Who am I?

In 2013, I travelled to Aruba for a 5-month internship at the island's leading tour operator De Palm Tours. As soon as I stepped outside the airport, I felt at home. Ever since, the island has a special place in my heart and I still spend a large part of the year on the One Happy Island.

As you can probably tell by taking a look at the other pages, I especially love hiking and exploring new areas of the island that even most locals do not know exist. My local friends often ask me to guide them around! I have been playing around with the idea of a website through which I can share my tips and secret spots, so that tourists can enjoy their vacation to the fullest, and go beyond the obvious tourist spots. I hope my page will inspire you to travel to Aruba, or give you some ideas for your vacation itinerary. Do you have any questions? Please do not hesitate to ask. I would love to help out!

Sunny greetings,
Sharon
